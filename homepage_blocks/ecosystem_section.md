## Part of the Literacy for Kids Ecosystem

This curriculum is part of the [Literacy for Kids](https://zcohen-nerd.github.io/literacy_for_kids/) project.

Literacy for Kids is a collection of open-source curricula designed to help children ages 8–12 understand the systems that shape modern life.

Each curriculum focuses on a foundational literacy:

- **[Decision Literacy](https://zcohen-nerd.github.io/decision_literacy_for_kids/)** — reasoning and choices
- **[Computer Literacy](https://zcohen-nerd.github.io/computer_literacy_for_kids/)** — understanding technology
- **[Media Literacy](https://zcohen-nerd.github.io/media_literacy_for_kids/)** — understanding information
- **[Financial Literacy](https://zcohen-nerd.github.io/financial_literacy_for_kids/)** — understanding money
- **[Civic Literacy](https://zcohen-nerd.github.io/civic_literacy_for_kids/)** — understanding governance

All curricula are free, open-source, and designed to work together as a unified learning framework.
